We purposely discount to attract and retain responsible and satisfied tenants (achieved by offering high quality, abnormally affordable and professionally managed homes).


Ottawa rates are normally $1.30-$1.50/sq ft (up to $2 for expensive luxury units).  Ours start at ~$1/ft2.

Our apartments are ~20-30% larger, ~10% cheaper and ~30-40% cheaper per square foot.
