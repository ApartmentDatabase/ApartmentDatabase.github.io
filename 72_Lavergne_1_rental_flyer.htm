A unique area, nestled between Rockcliffe Park luxury and Rockcliffe Cemetery forest. 


A quiet residential street in a great corner of the neighbourhood, two blocks from Beechwood Avenue, with a corner store ½ block away.

Lavergne is not a thru street to Beechwood, so it’s very quiet (with local traffic only).

It’s mostly owners on the block, so higher quality neighbours (than offsite landlords – as owners choose their neighbours carefully).

This also means better property care (as owners live onsite).

It’s more of a residential area than closer to the Bridge/Parkway, and the properties are also larger, which allow larger houses or duplexes/triplexes with larger, more spacious apartments.

We encourage happy, long-term tenants.  It's a standard 1-year lease, but our tenants stay 5, 10, 20 years.

We purposely discount to attract and retain responsible and satisfied tenants (achieved by offering high quality, abnormally affordable and professionally managed homes).
